Transfer programs

You can transfer after KCC and then complete a bachelor's degree. In fact, we guarantee it! With transfer agreements, you can finish your KCC degree then entered any four-year public university in illinois as a junior.
Learn more about our transfer programs

Occupational programs

These options get you ready for a career. Our programs and courses meet national accreditation standards. Many of these programs offer short-term options such as certificates which take from one semester to one year.
